Real Mexican Style Street Tacos!

These Mexican Street Tacos are the perfect dinner in a hurry and great for parties!

Ingredients

  • Uncooked Corn Tortillas
  • Protien of Choice
  • Seasoning
  • Cilantro
  • Avacado
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Grill Protien On A Hot Skillet
  2. Add Desired Amount Of Seasoning
  3. Chop Cilantro And Avacado
  4. 1tbsp Oil On A Hot Skillet Then Cook Tortillas On Each Side Until Lightly Brown
  5. Top Tortillas And Enjoy!
