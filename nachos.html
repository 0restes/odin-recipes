Delicious Over The Top Nachos!

These Nachos Will Have Your Guest Begging For More!

  • Ground Beef
  • Tortilla Chips
  • Jalapeno Peppers
  • Sharp Cheddar
  • Sour Cream
  1. Brown Ground Beef In Medium Sauce Pan
  2. Grate 2 Cups Sharp Cheddar
  3. Dice Jalapeno Peppers And Add To Ground Beef
  4. Spread Totilla Chips Evenly In Casrole Dish And Spread Cheese On Top
  5. Add Ground Beef Mix Topping And Bake 350 Degrees For 20 Minutes
