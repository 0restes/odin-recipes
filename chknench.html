Chicken Enchiladas

Delicious and quick chicken enchiladas that the family will love!

My favorite chicken enchilada recipe is easy to make, customizable with your favorite fillings, and made with my favorite homemade enchilada sauce. Its always a crowd fave, and perfect for meal prep and freezing too!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Enchilada Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Cream of Chicken Soup
  • Tortillas
  • Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil chicken
  2. Dice cooked chicken to desired size
  3. Place diced chicken in medium sauce pan with 1/2 can of cream of chicken soup and 1/2 can of enchilada sauce
  4. Grate 2 cups of cheese into sauce pan
